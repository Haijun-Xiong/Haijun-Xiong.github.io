About Me

I am now a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Bin Feng at Huazhong University of Science & Technology (HUST), School of Electronic Information and Communications (EIC). Before that, I received my B.Sc degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Huazhong University of Science & Technology, China, in 2022.

I major in Computer Vision and Deep Learning, and have published several papers about Gait Recognition on some venues. My past research scope involves Gait Recognition. The potential research I look forward to might be around Human-Centered Vision Tasks and Fine-grained Recognition Tasks, close to Representation Learning . Also, I'm open to discussing other potential directions. Please feel free to get in touch with me!
